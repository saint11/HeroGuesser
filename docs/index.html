    Basic Instructions

    Try to guess today's Dota 2 hero.

    Start by guessing a random hero, then look at the stats for hints!


    Red means incorrect.

    Yellow means partially correct.

    Green means correct.


    You can give up by typing "GG"


    Legs do NOT count the mount (horse, lizard), just the hero.

    Legs do NOT count hands, even if the hero stands on it. If it has opposing thumbs, it doesn't count.

    Good luck, have fun!
